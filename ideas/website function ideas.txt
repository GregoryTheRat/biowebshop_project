User registration: Customers can create an account on the website, which allows them to save their shipping and billing information for future purchases.

Product catalog: The website should display a list of available products along with their descriptions, prices, and images.

Shopping cart: Customers can add products to their cart, and view and modify their cart before completing their purchase.

Checkout process: Customers can securely complete their purchase online and choose their preferred shipping method.

Payment processing: The website should accept various payment methods such as credit card, PayPal, or other secure payment systems.

Order tracking: Customers can track the status of their order and receive updates on shipping and delivery.

Customer support: The website should provide customers with a way to contact customer support for questions or concerns about their order.

Newsletter subscription: Customers can subscribe to the website's newsletter to receive updates on new products, promotions, and other news.

Product reviews: Customers can leave reviews and ratings for products they have purchased to help other customers make informed decisions.

Rewards program: The website can offer a rewards program where customers can earn points for purchases and redeem them for discounts or free products.
